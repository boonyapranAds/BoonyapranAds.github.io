— About

The storyteller behind the lens.

I'm a filmmaker currently studying BA (Hons) Film & Television at the University of the Arts London, with a passion for storytelling that challenges perspectives and creates meaningful reflection.

My goal is to use film not just as entertainment, but as a tool to communicate ideas that stay with audiences long after the credits roll. I believe that storytelling can change people emotionally, socially, and intellectually.

Background & Experience

Over the course of my studies and personal film projects, I've taken on a variety of filmmaking roles including director, scriptwriter, editor, cinematographer, and even music producer. I co-directed and wrote for a full-length musical, sharpening my collaborative and narrative skills.

My diverse experience across different roles has given me a deep understanding of each element of film production. I'm always striving to sharpen the core message of the story. I'm passionate about learning on real sets, adapting quickly, and always bringing fresh, diverse perspectives to my work.
